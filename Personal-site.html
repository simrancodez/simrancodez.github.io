My Childhood

I was born in Columbia, South Carolina and when I was two my family moved to the Fort Mill area.

Music

One of my favorite things to do is listen to music! The best genres (in my opinion) are grunge, shoegaze, classic rock, R&B, jazz, and nu metal. My current favorite artists are Jeff Buckley, Elliot Smith, and Alice in Chains.

Books

I love to read classics and different feminist/dystopian novels! A few of my current favorites are The Color Purple, 1984, The Bell Jar, My Year of Rest and Relaxation, Song of Solomon, and Sula. It's a long list, but I reccomend anything Alice Walker, Toni Morrison, Sylvia Plath, Oscar Wilde, or Franz Kafka!
